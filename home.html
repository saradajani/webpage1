Computer Science Pathway

Description on Pathway:

Computer Science is one of the pathways offered at Da Vinci Communications to juniors and seniors. The Computer Science pathway was created to teach computer science in depth and gives students the opportunity to gain knowledge within the career field. The classes are taught by Eric Marintsch (below) and Andy Galbraith, with programming languages such as html, css, java, and python. With these coding languages the students in the Computer Science Pathway can create apps, websites, games, and build robots. Projects are usually done in groups, the class gets split up into groups of a certain number of individuals. Within these groups students break up the project/assignment into individual pieces and divide the tasks upon themselves. Towards the end the students put the pieces together and have a final piece. The class focuses on making real word decisions, making something useful function, rather than only testing the student on their knowledge of a certain language.
